🧠 Notes on Validation Logic

  • maxlength is applied to all varchar fields based on their defined size.

  • type="url" is used for fields expected to contain URLs (url, banner_image_url) for native validation.

  • type="number" with min and max reflects the range of smallint(6) (0 to 32767 for unsigned).

  • required is applied only where the schema or logic implies mandatory input (title, content_type).

  • Checkbox for status is mapped to a 3-character varchar, assuming values like 'on', 'off', or 'act'.
